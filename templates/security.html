Security & Trust

How Cosmic AI handles your data

Even though this is a demo environment, Cosmic AI is built with enterprise practices in mind. Below is a high-level view of how we think about security, privacy, and reliability.

Data in transit

All traffic to this demo application is served over HTTPS, ensuring your data is encrypted in transit between your browser and our servers.

Data handling

Uploaded documents are processed temporarily for conversion or summarization and then removed. We do not use your documents to train public models from this demo.

Access control

This demo is single-tenant and evaluation-focused. Enterprise deployments can include dedicated environments, SSO (SAML/OIDC), and role-based access control.

Compliance roadmap

For enterprise customers, we design deployments to align with common compliance frameworks such as GDPR, SOC 2, and ISO 27001. This includes clear data processing agreements, data residency options, and documented operational procedures.

Note: This public demo is not intended for production-sensitive data. For regulated workloads, please contact us about a dedicated environment with stricter controls.
